About Me

Alex (he/him)

Alex the owner of Woof Clothing went to Oakland Technical HighSchool. He has been interested in making clothing for dogs since 5th grade. In his senior year he started to make clothes for dogs in his room and sold them on the street. This led to him making a company called "Woof Clothing" and now sells clothes for dogs all over the world!
